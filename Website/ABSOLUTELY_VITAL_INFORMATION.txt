The Hills is a script typeface, perfect for logotypes. 
Designed by Måns Grebäck during 2017, this high quality lettering brings you to the sunny fifties. 
It is well balanced and has a nice medium weight. 

The font contains 350 glyphs and has support for a wide range of languages.
